# I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at the University of Arizona, advised by Dr. Judith L. Bronstein. 
#### I am interested in the impacts of environmental change on plant-pollinator communities. So far, these have been communities in the Rocky Mountains of Southwestern Colorado, since the Rocky Mountain Biological Laboratory has been my home away from home in the summers since 2023.
#### I am fundamentally a field ecologist, with no doubt because of my childhood spent rambling through the remnant blackland prairies and limestone creeks of North Texas, but also the summer I spent taking courses and doing fieldwork at 'Bug Camp', the University of Michigan Biological Station in Northern Michigan.
